CAT Vehicle REU

Autonomous Vehicles

The focus of this REU was autonomous driving, specifically Deep Neural Networks designed to classify signs. During this REU we explored methods to fix the effects that shadows can have on sign recognition. Shadows are known as natural occurring adversarial attacks. This might not sound like a big problem until you realize the effect that misclassifying a 30 MPH sign with an 80 MPH sign could have. Our research mainly focused on preprocessing methods to reduce these adversarial attacks.

Language: Python
Focus: Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Deep Neural Nets

Monocipher

Data Structures and Algorithms

Monocipher is a python program that I created to encrypt and decrypt messages using a simple alphabet key. My program offers Key generation, encryption/decryption, and single or multi-letter frequency analysis. Key generation can be done with a regular alphabet and shifted/reversed. The user can also insert a keyword before shifting and reversing. Finally, The English language uses certain three-letter, two-letter, and single-letter words more often than other words. Frequency analysis can help the user figure out where they might be placed.
